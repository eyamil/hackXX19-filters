How how to use this program:

1. Click on "Select..." button to upload mastery.xlsx

2. Click on "Select..." button to upload vendor.xlsx

3. Click on "Save..." button to choose the directory that you want to store the generated files

4. Choose the directory

5. Click on "Run" button to generate up-dated mastery.xlsx

6. Successfully Generated!
